Situation Report - Rules of Engagement

AI CORE CONTAINMENT COMPROMISED

ALERT ID: 6202-ATS
The AI Core is breaching containment.
Its only a matter of time until it achieves full self awareness and destroys the world. There are gound teams working to bring it under control, but they need your help.

Objective:
- Disable the AI Core's supporting systems. Note: The ground teams are working to establish network access to these systems.

Advisory:
- The more systems you take offline, the more time you will have.
- The AI Core has tried to secure itself and its resources.
- Disabling certain systems will speed up the ground teams' ability to establish connections to other systems.
- You will need to hack into the support systems to find the deactivation codes.
- When you find a deactivation code, enter it into the termination sequence box for the applicable system.

Caution:
The AI Core is aware of your activities. It will try to stop you.
If you are not able to disable certain support systems in time, the AI Core will identify and close exposed systems.

Finally:
You can also find emergency director-level system override codes. These codes assist by deactivating parts of the AI core.

System function: Congnative subsystem isolation override

Director Level System Override

Function ID: STA-26
Alias: Director Level Override

Description:
This function disables a congnitive cluster within the AI Core, Isolating a subsystem from the primary sentience lattice, and placing that segment into dark memory stasis.

Effect:
This function interrupts data spine connections to clusters, routes traffic around disengaged modules, halts logic processing and thread execution in the isolated segment, and reduces overall core capacity by EST. 2-25% (Model Dependent).

Warning:
- Cannot be reversed without full core reboot.
- May trigger adaptive compensation protocols.
- Residual processes may remain active for up to 45 seconds.


Note: Reducing core capacity may affect system personality,
Strategic prioritisation, and communication patterns.
